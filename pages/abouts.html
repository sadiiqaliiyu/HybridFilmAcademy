Hybrid Film Academy formally known as Goshen Film Academy was first established in 2007 in Benin City. Founded by Terapi Philip, Hybrid Film Academy is the result of a simple thought to introduce the art and craft of cinema to passionate minds.

HYBRID FILM AND MEDIA ACADEMY LTD (HYFA) is a multimedia and film training institute registered according to the laws of the Federal Fepublic of Nigeria in 2021 with its Headquarters in FCT Abuja, Nigeria.

Hybrid Film Academy unlike other film and media institute is inspired by the need to bridge the gap between aspiring filmmaking enthusiast and passion driven actors who really want to be part of the Nigeria movie industry by all means possible but lacks the capacity and professional know-how to do so, with top notch filmmaking practitioner who have zero tolerance for unprofessionalism.

We are working tirelessly to spread tentacles to more states and countries for easy access to aspiring filmmakers who would love to learn the craft of filmmaking. Periodically we offer special workshops in diverse location around Nigeria. These have included Lagos, Abuja, Benin, Anambra, and Enugu. We intend to organize more of this workshop across other states in Nigeria and beyond.
